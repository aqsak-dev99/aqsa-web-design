Santorini, Greece: 5-Day Romantic Escape

Best time: May–June or September–October

Experience the iconic white cliffs, volcanic beaches, and legendary sunsets of Oia. This itinerary blends relaxation, culture, and adventure.

Highlights:
Sunset in Oia Wine Tasting Boat Tour Black Sand Beach Hiking Caldera
Day-by-Day:
  • Day 1: Arrive in Fira, explore shops, sunset dinner
  • Day 2: Oia sunrise, photography walk, local winery
